# Page Copy Templates — Calgary Condo Search

These templates keep every page focused on Calgary condo lead generation. They are intentionally simple, clear, and conversion-driven.

## Global rules

- One clear H1 per page.
- Calgary-only contact information.
- Buyer pages push condo alerts or building alerts.
- Seller pages push condo value reports.
- Do not make unsupported claims about specific listings, MLS data, or building performance.
- Keep IDX/live listing content inside the approved myRealPage IDX experience.
