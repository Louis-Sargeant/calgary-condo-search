# Live Market Updates and Community Blog Plan

## Goal

Add live market updates and community blog content as core lead-generation assets.

These sections should build trust, improve Calgary condo SEO, create repeat traffic, and move visitors toward condo alerts, building alerts, market reports, and condo value reports.

## Live market update section

The site should include a dedicated Calgary condo market update area that can be updated regularly.

### Recommended page

```text
/calgary-condo-market-updates/
```

### Purpose

Help buyers and sellers understand what is happening now in the Calgary condo market.

### Content topics

- Calgary condo inventory updates
- price movement by area
- buyer demand by price range
- days on market trends
- condo fee concerns
- new listings activity
- sold activity summaries
- building-level observations
- downtown versus suburban condo demand
- luxury condo activity
- entry-level condo activity

### Lead actions

- Get Calgary condo market updates
- Request a condo value report
- Set up condo alerts
- Ask about a building

## Community blog section

The site should include community blog content connected to area pages.

### Recommended page

```text
/calgary-condo-community-blog/
```

### Purpose

Support area pages with useful buyer and seller content that answers real questions about Calgary condo living.

### Content topics

- Best Calgary condo areas for walkability
- Beltline condo living guide
- East Village condo living guide
- Mission condo living guide
- Bridgeland condo living guide
- Kensington condo living guide
- Downtown Calgary condo lifestyle
- Calgary condo buildings near restaurants and transit
- Calgary condo areas for first-time buyers
- Calgary condo areas for downsizers
- Calgary condo areas for investors
- Calgary condo areas with stronger resale appeal

## Content rules

- Do not write generic blog filler.
- Every post must connect to a search page, area page, building page, alert form, or value report.
- Every post must have a lead-capture CTA.
- Every post should answer a question buyers or sellers actually have.
- Keep content Calgary-only.
- Keep the tone practical and direct.

## Suggested publishing rhythm

Before launch:

- Build the market updates landing page.
- Build the community blog landing page.
- Prepare 6 initial community posts.
- Prepare 3 initial market update posts.

After launch:

- Publish one market update per week.
- Publish one community/blog article per week.
- Refresh top area pages monthly with market notes.

## Launch priority

These are important, but they should not delay the core IDX and lead-capture launch.

Priority order:

1. Core search and lead pages.
2. Condo alerts and building alerts.
3. Condo value report pages.
4. Market update landing page.
5. Community blog landing page.
6. First batch of blog/update posts.
