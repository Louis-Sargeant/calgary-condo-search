# Calgary Condo FAQ Shortcode

The `[ccl_faq]` shortcode adds a Calgary condo buyer FAQ section for SEO, objection handling, and lead conversion.

## What it does

- Answers common buyer questions before they leave the page.
- Builds trust by explaining what to check before buying a condo.
- Pushes serious buyers toward building research and condo alerts.
- Uses a mobile-friendly accordion layout.
- Keeps the content educational and does not add market statistics or listing data.

## Recommended placement

Place the FAQ after the checklist and buyer path sections, before the main alert form or near the bottom of SEO landing pages.

```text
[ccl_market_snapshot]
[ccl_building_checklist]
[ccl_buyer_path]
[ccl_faq]
[ccl_alert_form]
```

## Optional attributes

```text
[ccl_faq eyebrow="Calgary Condo Questions" title="What buyers should ask before choosing a condo" subtitle="Use these questions to separate the right Calgary condo from the wrong building, weak documents, bad rules, or poor resale fit."]
```

## Built-in FAQ topics

- What to check before buying a Calgary condo.
- How to compare condo fees.
- How to judge if a building is strong.
- Whether to search by area, building, or budget first.
- How building alerts help buyers move faster.

## Lead-generation purpose

This section is designed to keep visitors engaged after they browse IDX listings. It supports the conversion path from browsing to asking for guidance, building alerts, and document review help.

## QA before launch

1. Confirm each FAQ opens and closes cleanly on desktop and mobile.
2. Confirm the CTA jumps to the condo alert form.
3. Confirm the copy does not claim fake stats or guaranteed outcomes.
4. Confirm it appears only where it helps the page, not as filler.
