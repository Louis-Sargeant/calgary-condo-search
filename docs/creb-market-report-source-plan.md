# CREB Market Report Source Plan

## Decision

The Calgary Condo Market Report page should use CREB as the primary data source, not Google AI summaries and not competitor wording.

Correct source name:

```text
CREB® - Calgary Real Estate Board
```

## Primary source page

CREB Housing Statistics:

```text
https://www.creb.com/Housing_Statistics/
```

The CREB Housing Statistics page includes:

- Housing Data Map
- Daily Housing Summary
- Detailed Monthly Housing Reports
- City of Calgary reports
- Regional reports
- 2026 monthly reports

## Current report used for the first build

CREB City of Calgary Monthly Statistics Package, May 2026.

Key condo/apartment facts from the May 2026 package:

- Apartment sales: 403, down 29.79% year over year.
- Apartment new listings: 961, down 21.93% year over year.
- Apartment inventory: 2,070, down 1.15% year over year.
- Apartment months of supply: 5.14, up 40.80% year over year.
- Apartment benchmark price: $300,400, down 9.05% year over year.
- CREB states apartment-style conditions are favourable to buyers due to elevated supply and easing demand.

## How to use the data on our page

Use CREB numbers as factual market context, then add Louis's interpretation for buyers and sellers.

Do not copy CREB paragraphs. Summarize and cite/source CREB.

Use plain language:

- More supply means buyers have more choice.
- Sellers need sharper pricing and stronger presentation.
- Condo buyers should compare buildings, fees, parking, pet rules, exposure, and location before booking showings.
- Some property types and districts perform differently; avoid broad unsupported claims.
