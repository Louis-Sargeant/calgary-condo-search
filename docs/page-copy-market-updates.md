## Intro copy

The Calgary condo market changes by area, price range, building type, inventory level, and buyer demand. A single city-wide headline does not tell the full story. This page should help buyers and sellers track what is happening in the Calgary condo market and connect that information to better decisions.

For buyers, market updates help with timing, offer strategy, building selection, and alert setup. For sellers, market updates help with pricing, competition, and knowing how their condo compares to other active and recently sold units.
