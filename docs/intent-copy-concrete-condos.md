## Intro copy

Concrete condos are often preferred by Calgary buyers who want better sound separation, stronger building feel, higher-rise options, and long-term resale appeal. The construction type is only one part of the decision. Before booking a showing, compare the building, condo fees, reserve fund strength, parking, storage, amenities, bylaws, pet rules, and resale fit.
