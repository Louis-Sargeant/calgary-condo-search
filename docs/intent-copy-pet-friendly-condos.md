## Intro copy

Pet-friendly condos in Calgary can vary widely by building. Some buildings allow pets with board approval, some have size or number limits, and some are better suited to pet owners because of location, outdoor access, elevators, flooring, and nearby walking areas. Before booking a showing, compare the building rules, fees, parking, storage, layout, outdoor space, and resale fit.
