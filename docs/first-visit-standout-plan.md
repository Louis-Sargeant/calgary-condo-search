# First-Visit Standout Plan for Calgary Condo Search

## Goal

Make the site stand out immediately against other Calgary real estate lead generation sites.

The site should feel like a premium Calgary condo search engine with a real expert behind it, not a generic brokerage template.

## Above-the-fold requirements

The first screen must have:

1. Clear Calgary condo promise
2. Live IDX access visible quickly
3. Fighter-in-your-corner positioning
4. Two CTAs maximum
5. No clutter
6. No duplicate headers
7. No broken Elementor/plugin content

## Hero direction

Primary headline options:

- Search Calgary Condos With a Fighter In Your Corner
- Find the Right Calgary Condo Before You Book a Showing
- Calgary Condo Search Built for Serious Buyers

Primary CTA:

- Search Calgary Condos

Secondary CTA:

- Get My Condo Value Report

Optional trust line:

- Calgary condo search, building guidance, alerts, and value reports with one point of contact.

## Mobile-first lead funnel

Mobile visitors should immediately understand four actions:

1. Search condos
2. Get alerts
3. Compare buildings
4. Ask Louis

Use sticky or repeated CTAs carefully. Avoid covering the IDX filters too aggressively.

## Competitive advantage over Best Calgary Homes

Competitor has breadth. We win with focus.

Competitor: broad Calgary real estate site.
Our site: dedicated Calgary condo search and condo lead generation engine.

Competitor: many menu options.
Our site: guided path.

Competitor: generic team branding.
Our site: fighter-in-your-corner specialist branding.

Competitor: big content library.
Our site: build a cleaner, smarter condo-specific library.

## Visual rules

- Deep blue and gold brand palette
- Strong contrast
- Big readable text on mobile
- Cards should feel premium, not template-like
- No crowded menus above the fold
- No social feeds above lead capture
- No blog clutter above IDX

## Conversion rules

Every page must have one buyer CTA and one seller CTA:

Buyer CTA:

- Get Condo Alerts

Seller CTA:

- Get My Condo Value Report

Contact CTA:

- Talk to Louis

## Do-not-break rules

- Do not reactivate Calgary Condo Leads on the working Calgary Condos page.
- Do not edit the working Calgary Condos page with Elementor.
- Do not add multiple IDX shortcodes to the main page.
- Do not use heavy filtered IDX shortcodes until tested on a separate page.
- Do not rebuild all area pages at once.
