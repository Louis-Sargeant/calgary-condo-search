# Number One Calgary Condo Lead Gen Positioning

## Core position

This site is not a generic realtor website.

This site is a Calgary condo search engine and lead-capture platform built around:

- live condo search
- area-specific condo pages
- building-specific condo guidance
- CREB market data
- condo alerts
- condo value reports
- AI chat lead capture
- Louis's fighter-in-your-corner brand

## The winning angle

Most Calgary real estate sites are either:

1. generic brokerage pages
2. broad community directories
3. IDX search pages with little strategy
4. blog-heavy sites that do not guide the visitor
5. agent websites with weak calls to action

Our site should feel like:

```text
Realtor.com-style search utility + Calgary condo specialist + lead capture system
```

## First visit promise

Within five seconds, visitors should know:

- They can search Calgary condos here.
- They can narrow by area/building/lifestyle.
- They can get alerts.
- They can get a condo value report.
- They can ask questions through the Calgary Condo Assistant.
- A real expert is behind the site.

- Building guide

## AI chat role

The AI chat is a lead qualifier and search assistant, not a replacement for Louis.

It should:

- ask buyer/seller qualifying questions
- recommend next site path
- collect lead details
- route serious leads to Louis
- explain general condo concepts
- point to CREB market report and area pages

It should not:

- give legal advice
- give mortgage approval advice
- guarantee values or future prices
- pretend to be Louis
- claim access to live IDX facts unless integrated
