## Intro copy

Calgary condos with views can include skyline views, river views, mountain views, park views, or open exposure depending on the building and floor position. View quality can affect resale appeal, daily enjoyment, privacy, and long-term value. Before booking a showing, compare the building, exposure, floor level, balcony position, parking, storage, condo fees, amenities, bylaws, and resale fit.
