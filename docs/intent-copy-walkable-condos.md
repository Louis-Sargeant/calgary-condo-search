## Visual-first page direction

## Intro copy

Walkable Calgary condos are popular with buyers who want less driving and more convenience. The right location can put restaurants, coffee shops, groceries, transit, river pathways, parks, work, and entertainment close by. Buyers should still compare the building, condo fees, parking, storage, pet rules, noise, construction type, and resale demand before choosing a unit.
