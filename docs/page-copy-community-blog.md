## Intro copy

Calgary condo decisions are about more than listings. Buyers and sellers need area insight, building knowledge, lifestyle details, fee guidance, resale context, and market updates. This blog should connect Calgary condo communities, building types, buyer needs, and seller questions into one useful resource.

The goal is to help visitors understand where to search, what to compare, what questions to ask, and when to request alerts or a condo value report.
