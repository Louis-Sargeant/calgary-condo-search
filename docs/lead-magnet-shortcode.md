# Condo Buyer Lead Magnet Shortcode

The `[ccl_lead_magnet]` shortcode adds a premium checklist-style conversion block for Calgary condo buyers.

## Purpose

Some visitors are not ready to call or fill out a detailed form yet. This section gives them a softer conversion path by offering a Calgary condo buyer checklist and building-guidance angle.

## Default shortcode

```text
[ccl_lead_magnet]
```

## Optional attributes

```text
[ccl_lead_magnet eyebrow="Free Calgary Condo Buyer Checklist" title="Before you book showings, know what to check" subtitle="Get a sharper condo search plan focused on buildings, fees, rules, parking, storage, documents, and resale fit." button_text="Send Me The Checklist" button_url="#condo-alerts"]
```

## Recommended placement

Place this section after the FAQ or before the main alert form. It can also work near the bottom of area, price, and building landing pages.

```text
[ccl_building_checklist]
[ccl_buyer_path]
[ccl_faq]
[ccl_lead_magnet]
[ccl_alert_form]
```

## Conversion role

This section is designed to catch visitors who are still researching. It gives them a clear reason to request help before they are ready to book showings.

## Launch QA

1. Confirm the button jumps to the condo alert form or approved form section.
2. Confirm the section looks premium on mobile and desktop.
3. Confirm it does not promise guaranteed results or use fake statistics.
4. Confirm the page still has one clear lead path after this section.
