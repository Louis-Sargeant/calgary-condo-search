## Intro copy

Newer Calgary condos can attract buyers who want modern finishes, better amenities, improved layouts, newer mechanical systems, underground parking, and a cleaner move-in experience. But newer does not automatically mean better. Buyers should still compare the building, condo fees, warranty status, parking, storage, construction type, management, owner mix, and resale fit.
